Do I need Further Help?

Okay it appears you might need some further assistance. It might be a good idea to do a computer course to help you develop your computer skills. A friend or family member may be able to help you access some of the following supports.

  • Local Education and Training Board. A list of computer courses available.
  • Local citizens information service. To see if there are courses being run locally.
  • Microsoft Help MS Office or Windows help.
  • Apple Mac Computer Help. Have a look here for some basic guidance on using your equipment.
  • European Computer Driving Licence. Or 01-2377723 for further information.
  • ALISON Free online courses including computer skills.
