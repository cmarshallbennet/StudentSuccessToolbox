One of my first assignments involved creating a 5 minute PowerPoint presentation. I had never used PowerPoint and felt a bit nervous. However, I was able to get help because I knew how to search the internet for video tutorials about how to use Microsoft PowerPoint. I also checked the university website and found that there were introductory PowerPoint sessions available to students which really helped me. If I could give you one tip in relation to creating presentations it would be to check what help is available at your college before you begin and keep your presentation simple.
