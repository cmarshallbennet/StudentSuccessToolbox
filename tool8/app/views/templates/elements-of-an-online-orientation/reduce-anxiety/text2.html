Reduce Anxiety (‘Entry Shock’ ) Text 2

From graduate or current student perspective

Looking back to when I started I did feel anxious about starting on the course, about finding out what was expected of me. Was I going to be able to live up to the standards that I set for myself as well as the ones that others expected of me as well. It was hard to find a middle ground where you are comfortable, but it is good now I really do enjoy it. I just had to find out what was expected of me, and to be kind to myself in terms of meeting my own standards.

Mary (Student)

I've been out of school for so long that I was worried that I would stick out like a sore thumb but it was grand I was worrying about nothing. I just needed to make a plan and do the work. It all worked out.
