Activity 1 – Ice Breaker
  • Any Ice Breaker activity will do as long as it suits the context. Some examples:
    • Virtual Classroom – ‘Speed Dating’ Ice Breaker: Using breakout rooms (where you can group students so that they can talk just to each other in that group) place students in different pairs for three minutes at a time, such that they can have brief conversations with multiple peers
    • Virtual Learning Environments – Introduction Forum Ice Breaker: Using an ‘Introductions’ discussion Forum ask students to introduce themselves, upload a picture of their home town/where they live and say what is good about where they live.
