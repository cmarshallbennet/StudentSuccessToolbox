Reduce Anxiety

Why focus on reducing anxiety?

High Anxiety = Early Exit

High Anxiety = More start of year queries

Early experiences set the tone

Unresolved ‘Entry-Shock’ causes ongoing conflict

Possible Approaches

Provide accurate but reassuring messages

Demonstrate that anxieties are typical in new students

Bridge the gap between unrealistic expectations and reality

Focus attention on how success can and is achieved by students
