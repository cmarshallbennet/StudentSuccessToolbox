Positive Role Model - Activity 1

These activities can be led by a staff member or a trained student mentors/e-moderators

Virtual Classroom – Using breakout rooms (where you can group students so that they can talk just to each other in that group) place new students in a small group with a current student/trained student mentor such that the new students can ask questions of the current student and/or the current student talks about their experience of studying etc.

Virtual Classroom – Hold a guest webinar where one or more graduates and/or current students talk about their experience of studying etc., allowing some time for questions at the end of the webinar. If recorded some or all of the webinar can then become a video resource for those who could not attend.
