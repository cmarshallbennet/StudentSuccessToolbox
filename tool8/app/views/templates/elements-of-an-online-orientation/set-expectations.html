Set Expectations

Why focus on expectation setting?

Mismatched expectations = Early Exit

Unrealistic expectations = Early Exit

Mismatched expectations = More start of year queries

Unresolved mismatched expectations cause ongoing conflict

Possible Approaches

Provide explicit, accurate messages relating to

  • What students can expect from the institution and/or programme
  • What the institution and/or programme expects of students

Facilitate students in recognising what their (perhaps unconscious) expectations are

Bridge the gap between unrealistic expectations and reality

Present a set of positive expectations that new students should have about their studies
