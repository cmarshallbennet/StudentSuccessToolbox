Set Expectations - Activity

Some examples:

Virtual Classroom – use polls to present statements that are accurate or not accurate with regard to typical points of mismatch and get student feedback on how accurate they think the statement is (indicating degree of mismatch). And then use this as a basis for discussion of expectations, norms etc.

Virtual Learning Environments – Lead a discussion, using the discussion forum, to address typical mismatches in expectations and give a space for students to ask questions relating to the programme, what they should expect, and also what is expected of them. This discussion could begin with students viewing a video on this topic, or completing a quiz relating to typical points of expectation mismatch.
