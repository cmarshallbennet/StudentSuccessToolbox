Campus Tour - Activity

Virtual Classroom – Give students a virtual treasure or WebQuest in which to engage. Through this activity students will obtain information from key aspects of the virtual campus (e.g. support services webpages, library webpages etc.) and return to the virtual classroom with the information. This can also be conducted as a group exercise.

Virtual Learning Environments – In a discussion forum, give students a virtual treasure or WebQuest in which to engage. Through this activity students will obtain information from key aspects of the virtual campus (e.g. support services webpages, library webpages etc.).
