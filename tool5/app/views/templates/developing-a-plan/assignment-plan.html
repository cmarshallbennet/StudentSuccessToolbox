Plan

A good, well-organised plan is key. You should explore the steps involved in planning. The information here relates to a typical essay style assignment. You will have to adapt these steps for other types of assignments

Eamon (Tutor)
  • 1 Give yourself a timeframe
  • 2 Break down assignment question into searchable terms
  • 3 Have a clear argument or statement
  • 4 List main points within your argument
  • 5 Thinking about places to search first
