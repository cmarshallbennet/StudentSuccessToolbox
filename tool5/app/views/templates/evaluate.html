Evaluate

It is not much use to find information if you cannot tell whether it is good, bad or somewhere in between. You should explore the different elements of how to evaluate information. Remember that talking to a librarian, especially one who specialises in the subject area, is always a good idea when trying to evaluate the quality of a piece of information.
